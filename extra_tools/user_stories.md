# **🎯 CoActivity Platform \- Complete User Stories**

## **🔐 1\. AUTHENTICATION & ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT**

US-101: As a new user, I want to create an account  
*So that I can access all platform features*

US-102: As a user, I want to initiate login with credentials  
*So that I can receive a verification code for secure access*

US-103: As a user, I want to complete login with verification code

US-104: As a user, I want to securely log out  
*So that my session is properly terminated*

US-105: As a user, I want to permanently delete my account  
*So that I can remove all my data from the system*

US-106: As a new user, I want to verify my registration with a confirmation code  
*So that my account is activated securely*

US-107: As a user, I want to change my password  
*So that I can keep my account secure*

US-108: As a user, I want to reset my password if I lose access  
*So that I can recover my account without creating a new one*

US-109: As a user, I want to preview the consequences of account deletion  
*So that I understand what data and responsibilities will be removed*

US-110: As a user who owns rooms, I want to choose whether to delete my rooms or transfer ownership
before deleting my account  
*So that room communities are handled properly*

---

## **👤 2\. USER PROFILE MANAGEMENT**

US-201: As a user, I want to view my complete profile  
*So that I can see all my personal information*

US-202: As a user, I want to view other users' public profiles  
*So that I can learn about potential activity partners*

US-203: As a user, I want to update my profile information  
*So that I can keep my details current and accurate*

US-204: As a user, I want to configure notification settings  
*So that I control how and when I receive platform notifications*

US-205: As a user, I want to upload or update my profile avatar  
*So that other users can recognize me more easily*

US-206: As a user, I want to remove my profile avatar  
*So that I can control how much personal information I share*

---

## **🏠 3\. ROOM DISCOVERY & BROWSING**

US-301: As a visitor, I want to browse all public rooms  
*So that I can discover activities matching my interests*

US-302: As a visitor, I want to view detailed room information  
*So that I can make informed decisions about joining*

US-303: As a user, I want to see all my joined rooms  
*So that I can easily access my current activities*

US-304: As a user, I want to see rooms where I'm banned  
*So that I understand my participation restrictions*

US-305: As a visitor, I want to search rooms by keywords  
*So that I can quickly find relevant activities*

US-306: As a visitor, I want to filter and sort rooms by category, schedule, capacity, and
popularity  
*So that I can discover the most suitable activities faster*

US-307: As a visitor, I want to view room images  
*So that I can better understand the activity and its atmosphere before joining*

US-308: As a user, I want to see my current participation status for each room  
*So that I know whether I am a participant, have a pending request, or cannot join*

---

## **🎯 4\. ROOM CREATION & BASIC MANAGEMENT**

US-401: As a user, I want to create new rooms  
*So that I can organize activities I'm passionate about*

US-402: As a user, I want to join rooms  
*So that I can participate in activities that interest me*

US-403: As a user, I want to leave rooms  
*So that I can manage my activity commitments*

US-404: As a room owner, I want to delete my rooms  
*So that I can remove inactive or completed activities*

US-405: As a room owner, I want to edit room details  
*So that the activity description, schedule, and other information stay accurate*

US-406: As a room owner, I want to configure room privacy, participation rules, and capacity  
*So that the room fits the format of my activity*

US-407: As a room owner, I want to upload room images  
*So that I can present the activity more clearly and attract the right participants*

US-408: As a room owner, I want to remove room images  
*So that room media stays relevant and up to date*

US-409: As a room owner, I want to mark a room as completed or inactive without deleting it  
*So that participants understand the activity lifecycle and history is preserved*

---

## **⚡ 5\. ROOM ADMINISTRATION**

US-501: As a room administrator, I want to view room participants  
*So that I can manage the room community effectively*

US-502: As a room administrator, I want to verify user membership  
*So that I can confirm who has access to the room*

US-503: As a room owner, I want to promote users to administrators  
*So that I can share management responsibilities*

US-504: As a room owner, I want to demote administrators  
*So that I can adjust room management structure as needed*

US-505: As a room administrator, I want to update bulletin boards  
*So that I can communicate important information to all participants*

US-506: As a room participant, I want to view the bulletin board  
*So that I stay informed about important room updates*

US-507: As a room administrator, I want to remove participants from a room  
*So that I can enforce rules and resolve conflicts*

US-508: As a room administrator, I want to ban users from a room  
*So that I can protect the community from disruptive behavior*

US-509: As a room administrator, I want to view banned users  
*So that I can manage room access restrictions transparently*

US-510: As a room administrator, I want to unban users  
*So that I can restore access when appropriate*

US-511: As a room owner, I want to transfer room ownership  
*So that the room can continue when leadership changes*

US-512: As a room administrator, I want to delete outdated bulletin board posts  
*So that room communication stays relevant and clear*

---

## **📋 6\. JOIN REQUEST MANAGEMENT**

US-601: As a room administrator, I want to view all pending join requests  
*So that I can efficiently manage room access*

US-602: As a room administrator, I want to view room-specific join requests  
*So that I can focus on specific rooms I manage*

US-603: As a room administrator, I want to process join requests  
*So that I can control room membership and maintain quality*

US-604: As a user, I want to view my sent join requests  
*So that I can track the status of my applications*

US-605: As a user, I want to cancel pending join requests  
*So that I can withdraw applications I'm no longer interested in*

US-606: As a room administrator, I want to approve join requests  
*So that suitable users can become participants*

US-607: As a room administrator, I want to reject join requests  
*So that I can keep the room aligned with its purpose and rules*

---

## **❓ 7\. Q\&A KNOWLEDGE SYSTEM**

US-701: As a community member, I want to ask questions  
*So that I can get advice and help from experienced users*

US-702: As a community member, I want to answer questions  
*So that I can share my knowledge and help others*

US-703: As a visitor, I want to browse all questions  
*So that I can learn from community discussions and knowledge*

US-704: As a visitor, I want to view complete question threads  
*So that I can follow entire conversations and solutions*

US-705: As a visitor, I want to search and filter questions by category and keywords  
*So that I can quickly find relevant knowledge*

US-706: As a community member, I want to edit my questions  
*So that I can correct mistakes or improve clarity*

US-707: As a community member, I want to delete my questions  
*So that I can remove outdated or accidental posts*

US-708: As a community member, I want to edit my answers  
*So that I can improve or correct the information I shared*

US-709: As a community member, I want to delete my answers  
*So that I can manage my own contributions responsibly*

---

## **🔔 8\. NOTIFICATIONS & ACTIVITY UPDATES**

US-801: As a user, I want to receive email notifications about join request decisions  
*So that I always know whether I was accepted or rejected*

US-802: As a room administrator, I want to receive email notifications about new join requests  
*So that I can react quickly to membership changes*

US-803: As a room participant, I want to receive email notifications about important room updates
and closures  
*So that I do not miss critical information about my activities*

US-804: As a user, I want to see and manage which email notification categories are enabled in my
profile settings  
*So that I control what reaches my inbox*

US-805: As a user, I want email notifications to include enough room and action context  
*So that I can understand what happened and decide what to do next without confusion*
